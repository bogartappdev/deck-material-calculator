How this calculator works

The calculator estimates decking by dividing the deck width by the combined board width and gap, then converts the required linear footage into purchasable boards. Joists are estimated from the selected on-center spacing. Fasteners assume two screws at each board-to-joist intersection.

Before purchasing materials

Confirm the direction of your decking, available stock lengths, required beam and joist spans, footing depth, ledger details, and local permit requirements. Irregular shapes, picture framing, breaker boards, stairs, and diagonal decking require additional material.
