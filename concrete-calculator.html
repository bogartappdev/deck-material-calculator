HOW IT WORKS

Understanding your estimate

Rectangular pours use length × width × thickness. Round holes and columns use the volume of a cylinder. BuildPilot converts the result into cubic feet and cubic yards, then adds your selected waste allowance.

Builder’s tip

For larger pours, compare bagged-concrete labor and cost against ready-mix delivery. One cubic yard equals 27 cubic feet and may require roughly 45 eighty-pound bags.

When to add more waste

Consider 10–15% when excavations are irregular, the subgrade is uneven, forms may bow, or access makes spills likely.
